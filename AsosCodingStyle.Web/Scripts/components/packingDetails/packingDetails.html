Things you need to do now

Stick the label on the box

The returns label lets our customer service representative know that this box belongs to a very special person.

When they scan this label, they know what to expect in the box before they even open the box, so we can process the returns and refund you faster.

Put the items in the box

Remember to place all the items you want to return inside your box.

Please make sure items are in resaleable condition and have their original tags attached. We might reject items that are no longer in a resaleable condition.

Tape up the box

Couriers work magic and can get your parcel, and 1000s of others, from one side of the country to another overnight.

Make sure your box is sealed properly to avoid the contents falling out and getting lost or mixed up with the contents of other boxes in transit.

Take the box to the drop-off

Whether you're on your way home from work or on the way to buy your evening groceries, there are plenty of drop-off points available.

You can leave your box in your car and have a location-based reminder to remind you to drop it off next time you pass your local drop-off point.
